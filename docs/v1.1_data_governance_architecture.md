## Audit Chain

Each governance store maintains an append-only audit chain:

- Every write appends a record with `event_id`, `ts`, `prev_hash`, `hash`
- The `GovernanceStoreValidator` verifies chain integrity
- A broken chain triggers `ROLLUP_STORE_CORRUPTED` (P0 alert)
- Audit chain verification: `python main.py governance-rollup-audits`

---

## Qualification Levels

Symbols flow through three qualification levels:

| Level         | Meaning                                          |
|---------------|--------------------------------------------------|
| FORMAL        | Passed quality gate; eligible for formal backtest|
| OBSERVATIONAL | Data present but below formal threshold          |
| BLOCKED       | Missing, stale, conflicted, or invalid data      |

Qualification is assigned by `quality_gates/` and enforced by `gate_enforcement/`. The `governance_rollup/` module verifies consistency of these labels across modules.

---

## Safety Architecture

Every v1.1.x module enforces three safety layers:

1. **Module-level flags** in `__init__.py`: `NO_REAL_ORDERS=True`, `BROKER_DISABLED=True`, `TRADE_EXECUTION_ENABLED=False`
2. **Operation-level guards**: destructive operations require explicit `--allow-write` flag
3. **Stable checklist**: `stable-v060-check` verifies all safety flags on every release

The `CrossModuleConsistencyChecker` (v1.1.9) verifies that all module-level flags are present and correct.
